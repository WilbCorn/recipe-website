Nasi Goreng

Nasi goreng is the fried rice made from Indonesia. Full of spices, easy to make, 100% won't regret

Ingredients:

  • cooking spray
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • onion, chopped
  • 1 leek, chopped
  • 2 green chile peppers, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
  • ½ pound peeled and deveined shrimp
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 3 cups cold cooked white rice
  • 3 tablespoons kecap manis (sweet soy sauce)

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe isntructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
