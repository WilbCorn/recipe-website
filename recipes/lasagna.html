Lasagna

Lasagna is a type of pasta made from very wide, flat sheets. It is an italian dish.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe isntructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
